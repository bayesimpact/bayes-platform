Welcome! This help center gathers guides, walkthroughs, and answers to common
questions so you can get the most out of the platform.

## What you'll find here

- **Getting started** — set up your workspace and learn the basics.
- **Guides** — step-by-step instructions for everyday tasks.
- **Account & settings** — manage your profile, members, and preferences.
- **FAQ** — quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

## Finding an article

Use the search bar at the top of any page to look for a keyword, or browse the
categories from the home page. On desktop, press `/` to jump straight to search.

## Still need help?

If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out to your workspace
administrator or contact support from within the app.
