The **Admin** page is where a workspace's owner or admins manage its settings. It has
two tabs: **General** — rename the workspace — and **Conversation categories** —
create the labels used to classify your conversation agents' sessions.

You open it from **Admin** in the left sidebar of **Studio**. It applies to the whole
**workspace**.

## What conversation categories are

**Conversation categories** are workspace-level labels (for example *Product*,
*Pricing*, *Support*) used to **automatically classify** the sessions your
conversation agents have. Once a conversation agent has categories enabled, its
sessions are sorted into them, and the totals appear in the **Sessions per day by
category** chart in [Analytics](/en/analytics). Managing the list of categories
happens here in Admin; **turning them on for a given agent** happens on that agent's
**Conversation categories** tab.

## Step by step

### 1. Open Admin

In **Studio**, open **Admin** in the left sidebar. The page opens with a **General**
tab and a **Conversation categories** tab. (It's meant for workspace **owners** and
**admins**.)

### 2. Rename the workspace (General)

On the **General** tab, edit the **Workspace name** field and click **Save**. The new
name is used across the platform.

### 3. Manage categories (Conversation categories)

Open the **Conversation categories** tab. Your existing categories are shown as
badges; if there are none yet it reads **No categories yet.** Click **Add category**
to create one.

### 4. Add a category

In the **Add conversation category** dialog, type a **Category name**. Tick **Also
enable for all existing conversation agents** if you want every conversation agent to
start using it right away (otherwise you enable it per agent later). Click **Add**.

### 5. Remove a category

To delete a category, click the **✕** on its badge. A confirmation — **Remove
conversation category?** — asks before removing it from the workspace's category list.

### 6. Enable categories on an agent

Categories only classify an agent's conversations once they're **enabled on that
agent**. Open a **conversation agent**, go to its **Conversation categories** tab, and
tick the ones that apply (**Select all** adds them all). **Categories already used in
this agent's conversations cannot be unchecked.** Click **Save**. (See
[Add a conversation agent](/en/add-a-conversation-agent).)

## Tips

- Keep the category list **short and distinct** — a handful of clear labels classifies
  better than many overlapping ones.
- Tick **Also enable for all existing conversation agents** when a category should
  apply everywhere, to save enabling it agent by agent.
- After categories are in use, review the **Sessions per day by category** chart in
  [Analytics](/en/analytics) to see how conversations break down.

## Troubleshooting

- **I don't see the Conversation categories tab on an agent** — the tab only appears on
  **conversation** agents, and only once the workspace has **at least one** category.
  Create a category in Admin first.
- **The analytics chart shows only Uncategorized** — categories classify sessions only
  after they're **enabled on the agent**; enable them on the agent's tab and let new
  sessions come in.
- **I can't uncheck a category on an agent** — categories already **used** in that
  agent's conversations are locked on, so past sessions keep their labels.
- **I don't see the Admin page** — administering the workspace is reserved for its
  **owner** and **admins**; ask them if you need a change.
