Adding an admin means **inviting someone to your workspace** so they can work in it
with you. You invite people by their **email address**; once they accept, they
join as a **member** of the workspace.

Members are managed from the **Members** page in the left sidebar of **Studio**.
Members belong to the **workspace** — everyone you invite can collaborate on the
workspace's agents and resources.

## How members and invitations work

You invite someone by entering their **email address** and sending the invitation.
Until they accept, it stays listed under **Pending invitations**. Once accepted,
the person appears in the members grid with a **role badge** — **Owner** for the
workspace owner, **Admin** for the people you invite. You can remove a member, or
cancel an invitation that hasn't been accepted yet, at any time.

> **Roles are shown but not chosen here.** The invite dialog asks only for an email
> address — there is no role picker. The **Owner** / **Admin** labels on the cards
> are the platform's built-in role names and stay in English in every language.

## Step by step

### 1. Open Members

In **Studio**, open the left sidebar and select **Members**. The page is titled
**Members of** your workspace and lists everyone who already has access.

### 2. Start an invitation

Find the **Invite an admin** card and click **Invite**. This opens the invitation
dialog. (You can also search the existing members with the **Search by name or
email…** box at the top-right.)

### 3. Add the email address

1. In the **Invite an admin** dialog, type the person's address in the **Email
   address** field.
2. Click **Add**. The address appears as a chip below the field. Repeat to invite
   several people at once; remove a chip with its **✕** if you added one by mistake.

### 4. Send the invitation

Click **Send**. Each address you added receives an invitation to join the
workspace.

### 5. Track pending invitations

A sent invitation is listed under **Pending invitations** ("Invitations already
sent for this project") until the person accepts it. Each entry shows the invited
email and when it was sent.

### 6. See who has joined

Once someone accepts, they appear in the members grid with their email and a
**role badge** — **Owner** (crown) for the workspace owner, **Admin** (star) for
invited members. Click a member's card to open their detail page and review which
agents they can access.

### 7. Remove a member

To remove someone, click the **trash** icon on their card. A confirmation dialog
(**Remove *name*?**) explains the member will be removed from the project and that
the action **cannot be undone** — click **Remove** to confirm. You can't remove the
workspace **Owner** or yourself.

### 8. Cancel a pending invitation

To withdraw an invitation before it's accepted, click the **trash** icon on its
row under **Pending invitations** and confirm. This cancels the invitation that was
sent to that email.

## Tips

- Double-check each **Email address** before clicking **Add** — invitations go to
  exactly what you type.
- Invite several people in one go by adding multiple email chips before **Send**.
- Use the **Search by name or email…** box to find a member quickly in a large
  workspace.

## Troubleshooting

- **The person hasn't joined yet** — their invitation is still under **Pending
  invitations**. Ask them to check their inbox; if needed, cancel it and send a new
  one to the correct address.
- **I can't remove a member** — the **trash** icon isn't available for the workspace
  **Owner** or for your own account.
- **I invited the wrong address** — cancel the entry under **Pending invitations**,
  then invite the correct email.
- **I don't see the Members page** — you need access to the workspace's settings;
  ask the workspace **Owner** if it isn't visible.
