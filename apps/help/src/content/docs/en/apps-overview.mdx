A **workspace** is used through several **apps**, each for a different job. You open
them from your **workspace card** on the home page. Which apps you see depends on your
**role** and on what you've been **invited to** — most people only need one or two.

## The apps

- **App** — where you **use** the agents: chat with them, fill forms, and run
  extractions. Everyone with access to a workspace has it. See
  [Chat with an agent](/en/chat-with-an-agent).
- **Studio** — where **admins** build and configure the workspace: agents, sources
  (documents, web sources, resource libraries), members, categories and settings. Only
  users who can manage the workspace see it.
- **Evaluation** — the standalone app to **test extraction agents against a CSV
  dataset** and read a match rate. Shown when the feature is enabled. See
  [Evaluation dashboard](/en/evaluation-dashboard).
- **Test** — the **tester** experience: appears when you've been invited as a tester
  to a [review campaign](/en/review-campaigns). See
  [Participate as a tester](/en/participate-as-a-tester).
- **Review** — the **reviewer** experience: appears when you've been invited as a
  reviewer to a review campaign. See [Review as a reviewer](/en/review-as-a-reviewer).

## How to open an app

On the home page, each workspace has an **open** button. If you can access more than
one app, it's a **split button**: the main part opens your primary app, and the
**chevron** next to it opens a menu with every other app you can access. Pick the one
you need.

## Step by step

### 1. Open your workspace's apps

On the home page, find your **workspace card**. Click the main button to open your
primary app, or the **chevron** beside it to see every app you can access.

### 2. Studio

**Studio** is the admin/configuration side — building agents, adding sources, managing
members, categories and workspace settings. You'll see it only if you can manage the
workspace.

### 3. App

**App** is the everyday, end-user side — you talk to the agents an admin built, fill
forms, and run extractions. It's always available to workspace members.

### 4. Evaluation

**Evaluation** is a separate app for testing an **extraction agent** against a **CSV
dataset** and reading its accuracy. It appears when the feature is enabled for the
workspace.

### 5. Test

**Test** opens the **tester** app. It only appears once someone invites you as a
**tester** to a review campaign; there you chat with the agent and give feedback.

### 6. Review

**Review** opens the **reviewer** app. It only appears once someone invites you as a
**reviewer**; there you read testers' sessions and give your own independent rating.

## Tips

- If you only ever **use** agents, the one you want is **App**.
- If you **build** agents, you'll spend your time in **Studio**; the two open in
  separate tabs so you can keep both.
- **Test** and **Review** are invitation-only — they won't appear until a campaign
  owner adds you.

## Troubleshooting

- **I only see one button** — you have access to a single app; it opens directly with
  no menu.
- **I don't see Studio** — it's for workspace **admins/owners**; ask an admin if you
  need to configure something.
- **I don't see Test or Review** — those appear only after you're **invited** to a
  review campaign as a tester or reviewer (invitations arrive by email).
- **I don't see Evaluation** — it's an optional feature; ask your workspace **Owner**
  or an **Admin** if it should be enabled.
