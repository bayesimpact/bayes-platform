Adding a user means **inviting someone to use one of your agents**. You invite
people by their **email address**; once they accept, they join as a **member** of
that agent and can use it in the app.

This is different from adding a workspace collaborator — see [Add an admin](/en/add-an-admin)
for inviting people to the whole **workspace**. Here you grant access to a **single
agent**, from that agent's own **Members** page.

## How agent members and invitations work

Each agent has its own list of members. You invite someone by entering their
**email address** and sending the invitation. Until they accept, it stays listed
under **Pending invitations**. Once accepted, the person appears in the members
grid with a **role badge** showing their role (**owner** / **admin** / **member**).
You can remove a member, or cancel an invitation that hasn't been accepted yet, at
any time.

> **Roles are shown but not chosen here.** The invite dialog asks only for an email
> address — there is no role picker. The role labels on the cards are the platform's
> built-in role names.

## Step by step

### 1. Open the agent

In **Studio**, open the agent you want to give access to — either from the
**Agents** group in the left sidebar or from its card on the workspace overview.

### 2. Open the agent's Members page

On the agent's page, click **Members** in the top-right actions (next to **App** and
**Edit**). The page is titled **Members of** your agent and lists everyone who
already has access, with the invite card at the end of the grid.

### 3. Start an invitation

Find the **Invite a user** card and click **Invite**. This opens the invitation
dialog.

### 4. Add the email address

1. In the **Invite a user** dialog, type the person's address in the **Email
   address** field.
2. Click **Add** (or press **Enter**). The address appears as a chip below the
   field. Repeat to invite several people at once; remove a chip with its **✕** if
   you added one by mistake.

### 5. Send the invitation

Click **Send**. Each address you added receives an invitation to use the agent.

### 6. Track pending invitations

A sent invitation is listed under **Pending invitations** ("Invitations already
sent for this agent") until the person accepts it. Each entry shows the invited
email and when it was sent.

### 7. See who has joined

Once someone accepts, they appear in the members grid with their email and a
**role badge** showing their role (for example **member**).

### 8. Remove a member

To remove someone, click the **trash** icon on their card. A confirmation dialog
(**Remove *name*?**) explains the member will be removed from the agent and that
the action **cannot be undone** — click **Remove** to confirm. You can't remove the
agent **owner** or yourself.

### 9. Cancel a pending invitation

To withdraw an invitation before it's accepted, click the **trash** icon on its
card under **Pending invitations** and confirm. This cancels the invitation that
was sent to that email.

## Tips

- Double-check each **Email address** before clicking **Add** — invitations go to
  exactly what you type.
- Invite several people in one go by adding multiple email chips before **Send**.
- Agent members are scoped to that agent only. To give someone access to the whole
  workspace instead, use [Add an admin](/en/add-an-admin).

## Troubleshooting

- **The person hasn't joined yet** — their invitation is still under **Pending
  invitations**. Ask them to check their inbox; if needed, cancel it and send a new
  one to the correct address.
- **I can't remove a member** — the **trash** icon isn't available for the agent
  **owner** or for your own account.
- **I invited the wrong address** — cancel the entry under **Pending invitations**,
  then invite the correct email.
- **I don't see the Members button** — you need management access to the agent; ask
  the agent **owner** or a workspace **admin** if it isn't visible.
