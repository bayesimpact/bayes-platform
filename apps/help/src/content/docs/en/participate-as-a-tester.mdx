When someone invites you as a **tester** to a **review campaign**, you help evaluate
an agent by having real conversations with it and giving structured feedback. You do
this in the **Test** app: chat with the agent, rate each session and answer a few
questions, and — when you're done — fill in an end-of-phase survey.

The **Test** app opens from your **workspace** card on the home page. Its button
(with a checklist icon) only appears once you've been invited to test a campaign in
that workspace.

> This guide is for **testers**. If you were invited as a **reviewer** (to read and
> judge other testers' sessions), see [Review as a reviewer](/en/review-as-a-reviewer).
> If you're the person **setting up** the campaign, see
> [Review campaigns](/en/review-campaigns).

## How it works

- A campaign points at one **agent**. As a tester you start **sessions**
  (conversations) with it.
- After each session you give **per-session feedback**: an overall **star rating**
  (required), an optional comment, and the campaign's questions.
- Each session shows a **status** — *Feedback pending*, *Feedback submitted*, or
  *Abandoned*.
- When you've tested enough, you **finish participating** and fill an optional
  **end-of-phase survey** (an overall rating + questions about the whole experience).
  You can keep testing and edit your survey until the campaign closes.

## Step by step

### 1. Open the Test app

On the home page, find your **workspace** card and open its **app menu** (the chevron
next to the main button). Choose **Test** — the entry with the checklist icon. (If
you don't see it, you haven't been invited to a campaign in that workspace yet.)

### 2. Open your campaign

The **My review campaigns** page lists the campaigns you've been **invited to test**,
each with the note *Invited to evaluate this agent*. Click a campaign to open it. If
you have none yet, it shows **No active review campaigns**.

### 3. Start a conversation

The campaign page shows the agent (its name and type, e.g. **Conversation agent**,
and its greeting). Click **Start a conversation** to begin a new testing session.

### 4. Chat, then end the session

You're now in a normal chat with the agent — ask questions and interact as a real
user would. A **Review campaign** badge and the agent's name appear in the header.
When you've seen enough, click **End session & give feedback**.

### 5. Give your feedback

A dialog — **How was this session?** — asks for:

- **Overall rating** — a 1–5 star rating (**required**).
- **Comment** — optional free text.
- The campaign's **per-session questions** — these can be a star **rating**, a
  **single choice** (pick one option), or **free text**. Questions marked with a red
  **\*** are required.

Fill it in and click **Submit feedback**. (You can also reopen a **Feedback pending**
session later from the list and use **Give feedback**.)

### 6. Track your sessions & finish

Back on the campaign page, **Your past sessions** lists every session with its
**status** badge (**Feedback submitted** / **Feedback pending** / **Abandoned**) and
actions: **Open** to revisit it, **Give feedback** if still pending, or the **trash**
icon to delete a pending session. When you've tested enough, click **Finish
participating** and confirm **Go to the survey**.

### 7. Complete the end-of-phase survey

The **End-of-phase survey** asks for an overall **rating** (required), an optional
**comment**, and any end-of-phase questions. Click **Submit survey**. Afterwards the
campaign shows **Participation finished** — you can still **Edit my survey** (and
start more sessions) until the campaign closes.

## Tips

- The survey is **optional** — your per-session feedback already counts. But it's the
  best place to summarise your overall impression.
- Start a **fresh session** for each scenario you want to test, so each gets its own
  rating and feedback.
- You can leave a session and come back: pending sessions stay in **Your past
  sessions** with a **Give feedback** button.

## Troubleshooting

- **I don't see the Test app** — its button only appears after you've been **invited
  as a tester** to a campaign in that workspace. Ask the campaign owner to invite you
  (invitations arrive by email).
- **The campaign isn't in my list** — only **active** campaigns you were invited to
  appear. A campaign that hasn't been activated yet, or has closed, won't show.
- **I can't submit my feedback** — the **Overall rating** and every question marked
  **\*** are required; fill them in to enable **Submit feedback**.
- **I finished by mistake** — you can still **Edit my survey** and **Start a
  conversation** until the campaign is closed by its owner.
