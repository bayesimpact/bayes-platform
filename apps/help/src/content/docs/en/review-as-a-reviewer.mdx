When someone invites you as a **reviewer** to a **review campaign**, you read the
sessions that **testers** had with an agent and give your own independent judgement.
Reviews are **blind**: the tester's rating and comment stay hidden until you submit
yours, so your judgement isn't influenced. You do this in the **Reviewer** app, and
you can also consult the campaign's aggregated **report**.

The **Reviewer** app opens from your **workspace** card on the home page. Its button
(with a glasses icon) only appears once you've been invited to review a campaign in
that workspace.

> This guide is for **reviewers**. If you were invited as a **tester** (to chat with
> the agent), see [Participate as a tester](/en/participate-as-a-tester). If you're
> **setting up** the campaign, see [Review campaigns](/en/review-campaigns).

## How it works

- A campaign has **testers' sessions** waiting to be reviewed. You open one, read its
  **transcript**, and submit **Your review**: an overall **star rating** (required),
  an optional comment, and the campaign's reviewer questions.
- **Blind review** — until you submit, the tester's rating/comment are hidden. You
  only see the objective **Session facts** and the transcript. After you submit, the
  **Tester feedback** and **Other reviewers** become visible, and you can still edit
  your review (**Save changes**).
- You **can't review your own** tester sessions — those rows are marked *your session*
  and their action is disabled.
- The **Campaign report** aggregates everything: mean ratings, per-question
  distributions, and a per-session matrix, with **Download CSV**.

## Step by step

### 1. Open the Reviewer app

On the home page, find your **workspace** card and open its **app menu** (the chevron
next to the main button). Choose **Review** — the entry with the glasses icon. (If you
don't see it, you haven't been invited to review a campaign in that workspace yet.)

### 2. Open your campaign

The **My review campaigns** page lists campaigns where you're a reviewer, each with
the note *Review tester sessions and submit your own rating*. Click one to open it. If
you have none yet, it shows **No review campaigns yet**.

### 3. Pick a session to review

The campaign page shows the agent and **Sessions to review** — a table with columns
**Session**, **Started**, **Messages**, **Reviewers**, and **My review** (**Pending**
or **Submitted**). Click **Review** on a pending session (or **View** on one you've
already done). Rows marked *your session* are disabled — you can't review your own.

### 4. Read and submit your review (blind)

The session opens with two panes:

- On the left, the **Session facts** (objective answers the tester gave) and the
  **Transcript** of the conversation. A banner reminds you that the tester's rating
  and comment are **hidden until you submit** — this keeps your judgement independent.
- On the right, **Your review**: an overall **Your rating** (1–5 stars, **required**),
  an optional **Comment**, and the campaign's reviewer questions (rating / single
  choice / free text). Fill it in and click **Submit review**.

### 5. See the revealed feedback

Once you've submitted, the review is no longer blind. The **Tester feedback** (their
overall rating and comment) and any **Other reviewers** appear on the left. Your own
review stays editable — adjust it and click **Save changes** if needed.

### 6. Open the campaign report

From the campaign page, click **Campaign report** (top right) to see the aggregated
results across all sessions and reviewers.

### 7. Read the report

The report shows headline stats — **Sessions**, **Mean tester rating**, **Mean
reviewer rating**, **Mean end-of-phase** — a per-question breakdown, and a **Session
matrix** (per session: tester, reviewers, mean reviewer rating and spread). Use
**Download CSV** to export the raw data.

## Tips

- Form your opinion from the **transcript and Session facts first** — that's the point
  of the blind review. The tester's rating only appears afterwards.
- Answer every reviewer question thoughtfully; your responses feed the report's
  per-question breakdown.
- Compare your rating with **Other reviewers** (visible after you submit) to see where
  judgements agree or differ.

## Troubleshooting

- **I don't see the Reviewer app** — its button only appears after you've been
  **invited as a reviewer** to a campaign in that workspace (invitations arrive by
  email). Unlike testers, reviewers keep access to **closed** campaigns too.
- **The Review button is disabled on a row** — that's **your own** tester session
  (marked *your session*); reviewers can't review their own sessions.
- **I can't submit / edit my review** — an overall **Your rating** plus every required
  question is needed to submit. If the campaign is **closed**, sessions become
  **read-only** and can no longer be edited.
- **The tester's rating is hidden** — that's intentional (**blind review**); it appears
  once you submit your own review.
- **Related:** the sessions you review come from [testers](/en/participate-as-a-tester),
  and the campaign itself is set up in [Review campaigns](/en/review-campaigns).
