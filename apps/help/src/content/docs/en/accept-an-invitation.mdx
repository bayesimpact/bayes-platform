You're usually added to the platform by **invitation**: someone invites you to a
**workspace**, an **agent**, or a [**review campaign**](/en/review-campaigns), and you get an email. This
guide covers your first sign-in — accepting the **terms**, then finding and
**accepting** your invitations from the home page.

## The first sign-in

1. Open the **invitation email** and click its link.
2. **Sign in** (or create your account) when prompted.
3. The very first time, you're asked to **accept the terms** before you can continue.
4. You land on the **home page**, where your **pending invitations** are waiting.

## Step by step

### 1. Accept the terms

On your first sign-in, a **Terms & Compliance** screen asks you to review and accept a
few documents. Tick:

- **I accept the General Conditions of Service (required)**
- **I accept the Privacy Policy (required)**
- **I accept the AI Systems Usage Policy (optional)**

Use the **Open** link next to each to read the document. The **Accept and continue**
button stays disabled until both **required** boxes are ticked. Click it to enter the
platform.

### 2. Find your pending invitations

On the home page (**Welcome, …!**), a **Pending invitations** section lists everything
you've been invited to, with the description *Invitations waiting for you to accept.*
Each card shows a badge for the type — **Project** (a workspace), **Agent**, or
**Review campaign** — the name, the organization, and your **Role**. The list
**refreshes on its own**, so a new invitation appears without reloading the page.

### 3. Accept an invitation

Click **Accept** on an invitation. You immediately join that workspace, agent or
campaign with the role you were given.

### 4. Open your new workspace

Once accepted, the item moves out of the invitations list and the workspace appears
under its **organization** below, with its open button — ready to use. See
[Apps overview](/en/apps-overview) for what each app does.

### 5. Rename your organization (owners & admins)

If you **own or administer** an organization, its card shows a **pencil** next to its
name — click it to rename the organization. (Members without that role won't see the
pencil.)

## Tips

- No invitation yet? Ask the person setting things up to invite your **email address**
  — invitations are sent to a specific address.
- You can also **edit your display name** later from the user menu — see
  [Manage your profile](/en/manage-your-profile).
- Not sure which app to open after joining? See [Apps overview](/en/apps-overview).

## Troubleshooting

- **The Accept and continue button is greyed out** — you must tick both the **General
  Conditions** and the **Privacy Policy** (the AI usage policy is optional).
- **I don't see my invitation** — it's sent to a specific **email address**; make sure
  you signed in with that address. The list also refreshes automatically, so give it a
  moment.
- **My invitation disappeared** — an invitation that was **revoked** (or already
  accepted) drops off the list. Ask the sender to invite you again if needed.
- **I have no workspaces and no invitations** — you'll be prompted to **create an
  organization** (available to eligible accounts); otherwise ask to be invited to an
  existing workspace.
